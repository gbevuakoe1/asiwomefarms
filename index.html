We've got what you need

ASIWOME Farms is an agribusiness venture that specializes in livestock like grasscutters and poultry related activities. We are a leading producer of fresh, healthy and quality table eggs for your consumption. ASIWOME Farms is located at Kopeyia in the Ketu South Municipality of Volta Region, Ghana. We also provide dressed chicken (layers and broilers). Our team of great professionals engage in agribusiness and provide training and consultancy services to individuals and institutions.

Services

Eggs Production

Egg production is our core business. We produce fresh, high-quality eggs with rich yellow yolks all year round, supplying wholesalers, retailers, and individuals nationwide at competitive prices.

Broilers and Layers

Enhance your dishes with our freshly dressed layers, and choose our healthy, tasty, low-fat broilers for nutritious meals.

Agribusiness and Consultation

At ASIWOME Farms, we provide advisory, consultancy, and training services to promote best farming practices. We have helped many individuals start and successfully run their poultry farms. We also teach proper record keeping and offer an easy-to-use software tool that helps farmers track their operations clearly and consistently.
